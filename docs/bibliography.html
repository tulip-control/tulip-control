R. Ehlers (2010). Generalised Rabin(1) synthesis, CoRR, abs/1003.1684

E.A. Lee and S.A. Seshia. Introduction to Embedded Systems - A Cyber-Physical Systems Approach. LeeSeshia.org, 2011.

[WTM12]

T. Wongpiromsarn, U. Topcu, and R. Murray (2012). Receding horizon temporal logic planning. IEEE Transactions on Automatic Control, 57(11):2817–2830.

[WTOXM11]

T. Wongpiromsarn, U. Topcu, N. Ozay, H. Xu, and R. Murray (2011). TuLiP: a software toolbox for receding horizon temporal logic planning. In Proc. of Hybrid Systems: Computation and Control (HSCC).
